How to avoid getting hacked/scammed.

Are you worried about hackers getting access to your email, social media or bank accounts? You can follow these 5 tips to keep your accounts safe and secure. If you get this stuff sorted, you're already drastically reducing your chances of being hacked without having to put much work in.

Backup Important Data.

The main reason for data backup is to save important files if a system crashor hard drive failure occurs. You should back-up important data on an external hard drive or USB stick. If there’s something that you don't want to lose, keep copies!

Log in to accounts from your own devices.

The main way people get hacked is by leaving important accounts on public devices like a library computer and forgetting to log off. You may also let google save the passwords, and people can login to your account after you have left. If you don't want that to happen, then you should follow these tips.

  • Never forget to log off of a computer
  • Never let google save anything to a device unless it's your personal one
  • Always watch your back when inserting things like passwords
  • Never tell your password to ANYONE.

Get a decent anti-virus and firewall software!

You should get an Anti-Virus and a Firewall software so then it would be harder for hackers to hack your computer, it may be a small change and a hassle for some, but trust me it is worth it because it will be a guard for you and make it even harder for the hackers to get to your private personal information.

Try not to use the same password for different accounts.

So if you use the same password for multiple websites it will be easier to hack you since it's just one password they memorize and if you have the same password for a password manager it will be even easier to hack you since they know all of your passwords and every site they're used on so if you want to avoid getting hacked change your password.

Don't post everything online!

It is important that you don't post everything online because people from across the globe can see what you posted. I'm not saying that you shouldn't post anything online, I am just saying don't post really personal things online as public, at least put it on private so only friends or family can see.
